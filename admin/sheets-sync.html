Google Sheets Setup Guide

  1. Design your spreadsheet headers exactly as listed below.
  2. Go to File > Share > Publish to web.
  3. Choose Link, change dropdown to Comma-separated values (.csv).
  4. Click Publish, copy the link, and paste it here.
