Help Center

Find immediate answers to operational and system policies

💍 How do I request token access to premium collections?
Navigate to "Token Requests" from the sidebar and click "Request New Token". Alternatively, view any restricted product detail card and click the "Request Token" button. Your request will be evaluated by an administrator and activated for a selected duration.
📦 What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
Our minimum order quantity is exactly 1 piece per design for approved dealers, providing maximum catalog flexibility.
🔐 How does device fingerprint tracking work?
To protect our exclusive designs, each dealer token is bound to a limited number of browser sessions. If you reach your limit, you can manage and delete active device bindings under your Profile.
💬 How can I share products with customers?
Bookmark designs into your "My Selections" panel, review the compiled order inquiry details, and click "Open WhatsApp" to share the formatted catalog interest message directly with our dispatch desk.
